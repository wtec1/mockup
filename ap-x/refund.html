Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

At Wtec1 Technologies, we strive to provide high-quality educational services through WTEC1 LMS and Eduvault. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds may be issued. Please read this policy carefully before making a payment.

1

General Refund Policy

No Refunds for Standard Subscriptions

As a general rule, all payments made for subscriptions to WTEC1 LMS and Eduvault are non-refundable. This includes:

Monthly subscription fees

Subject package purchases

Any additional content or resource fees

Unused days or partial months of service

2

Exceptions: Technical Issues

Refunds may be considered in the following exceptional circumstances:

Platform Access Failure

If you are unable to access the platform due to technical issues on our end for more than 7 consecutive days, and our support team cannot resolve the issue.

Duplicate Payments

If you were charged multiple times for the same subscription period due to a system error.

Unauthorized Transactions

If a payment was made without your authorization and you report it within 48 hours with proper documentation.

3

Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting support.

Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.

You will continue to have access to all content until the end of the paid period.

No refunds will be provided for the remaining days in your subscription period after cancellation.

4

How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund under our exceptional circumstances:

1

Contact Support

Reach out to our support team via email at info@ac.wtec1.xyz or through our WhatsApp support group.

2

Provide Details

Include your account information, payment receipt, transaction reference number, and a clear explanation of the issue.

3

Wait for Review

Our team will review your request within 5-7 business days and respond with a decision.

4

Refund Processing

If approved, refunds will be processed within 10-14 business days to the original payment method.

5

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be granted in the following cases:

Change of mind or dissatisfaction with content quality

Failure to use the platform or access materials during your subscription period

Internet connectivity issues on your end

Device compatibility issues (ensure your device meets our technical requirements)

Account suspension or termination due to violation of Terms of Service

Requests made more than 30 days after the payment date

6

Partial Refunds

We do not offer partial refunds for unused portions of a subscription period. Once a payment is made, you will have access to the platform for the full period you paid for, regardless of usage.

7

Changes to This Policy

Wtec1 Technologies reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our platforms after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

8
